<Steps>

### Enable community plugin

1. Open `Settings` > `Community plugin`
2. Turn on community plugin

### Install

1. Open [plugin page](https://obsidian.md/plugins?id=zotlit) in obsidian
2. Click `Install`
3. Wait for installation to complete

### Enable

Click `Enable` Button
